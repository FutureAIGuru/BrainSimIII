This is important information about the fine tuning datasets.

common_words_ds_12.jsonl
- provides 12 examples of is-a and some other relationships.

common_words_ds_50.jsonl
- provides 50 examples similar to the 12 dataset.

word_only_dataset.jsonl
- provides 20 examples of is-a relationships only.

clause_dataset.jsonl
- [WORK IN PROGRESS]
- provides 20 examples of IF clauses for common nouns.

words_extracted.txt
- provides the 5000 most common words from Simple Wikipedia.
